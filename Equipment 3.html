Here are some of the ways CCTV is used for security

Crime prevention: CCTV can help prevent crime by monitoring premises and recording footage of events.

Evidence: CCTV footage can be used as evidence in case a crime is committed.

Access control: CCTV can be used to control access to a specific area.

Security regulations: CCTV can be used to ensure that security regulations are being followed.

Home security: CCTV can be installed in homes to ensure the safety of loved ones.

CCTV can also be used for other purposes, such as: Monitoring traffic, Monitoring public spaces, Monitoring industrial processes, Managing vehicle fleets, and Analyzing customer behavior patterns.

CCTV systems can use both analog and digital technology, and can record 24/7. The footage can be viewed in real-time on a monitor or mobile device, or recorded and reviewed later.
