Garret super scanner V

Garrett metal detectors have search modes for specific items, such as coins, jewelry, or all metal.

ADJUSTABLE FREQUENCY

Some Garrett metal detectors have adjustable frequencies to reduce interference from other metal detectors or electrical sources.

ELECTRONIC PINPOINTING

Some Garrett metal detectors can pinpoint the exact location of a detected target.

DEPTH INDICATOR

Some Garrett metal detectors can indicate the depth of a target.

IRON AUDIO

Some Garrett metal detectors can help you hear iron targets that have been discriminated.

SENSITIVITY ADJUSTMENTS
Some Garrett metal detectors have sensitivity adjustments to help you adapt to different environments.
TARGET ID

Some Garrett metal detectors have a digital target ID that shows what's below the detector.

CAMLOCKS

Some Garrett metal detectors have camlocks to help stabilize the detector.

LOW BATTERY INDICATOR

Some Garrett metal detectors have a low battery indicator to remind you when it's time to charge.

SUBMERSIBLE SEARCH COIL

Some Garrett metal detectors have a submersible search coil so you can use them in water.

Garrett also makes metal detectors for security purposes, including walk-through detectors and hand-held wands. These detectors can help screen for weapons and other metal objects.
